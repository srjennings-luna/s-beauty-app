Verdigris · verdigris → gilt

What each pigment says. Mauve reads as dusk, drapery, Sehnsucht — the longing register of Beauty. Verdigris reads as aged copper, olive grove, patina — the time-and-tradition register of Truth and Suffering.

Reference fit. Verdigris pairs with ochre/gilt in the Variant “Verdigris & Ochre” reference you pulled (Vatican Stanze drapery). That pairing is one of the most documented Renaissance fresco combinations — actual copper-acetate pigment used alongside earth yellows for centuries.

Differentiation. Both move firmly away from sacred gold flat. Mauve is the warmer, more devotional register; verdigris is the cooler, more art-historical register. Neither resembles any current AI-meditation app.
