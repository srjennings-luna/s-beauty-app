Option 1 · Whisper

Mauve-tinted espresso

No full mauve anywhere. The top is espresso with a 30% mauve undertone, fading to pure espresso by mid-screen. Reads as espresso with warmth — a hint, not a wash.

background: linear-gradient(180deg, #3A2D2E 0%, #16110D 50%);

Option 2 · Dawn band

Full mauve, top fifth only

Keeps the saturation of the original but compresses it into the top 20%. Below that, pure espresso. Like a dawn band above a dark wall.

background: linear-gradient(180deg, #8E6E7B 0%, #16110D 22%);

Option 3 · Soft wash

Half-strength mauve, slow fade

Half-strength mauve at top, gentle transition through a deeper mid-tone, settling into espresso by three-quarters down. More even than Option 2, more present than Option 1.

background: linear-gradient(180deg, #523F44 0%, #2A1F22 35%, #16110D 75%);
